Job Description
About the role
SWIFT is looking for experienced Java developers/software engineers (full-stack engineers) who excels at creating an exceptional user experience. Based in our Kuala Lumpur office, you will be working alongside a dynamic team who are fully responsible for end to end delivery - from customer engagement to operations. As a driving force of technological change, we operate in a true agile way. You will have a unique opportunity to expand your reach in terms of knowledge, network and build your expertise through SWIFT's global platform. We want the right people. If you've got what it takes we will have a role for your level of expertise.
What to expect
  • You will design, develop, test (including automated integration and regression) and integrate new features, components and improvements to compliance products.
  • You will use a range of proven technologies, (Java/JavaScript on both Linux & Window environment) with lots of room of experimentation (for example, container orchestration, CI/CD tooling, distributed architectures like ElasticSearch) to drive true value to our customers.
  • You will have the opportunity to develop yourself and others in a true agile environment. Covering software architecture, development, quality assurance, security, operations and IT infrastructure aspects.
  • You will work in a truly international environment with main hubs across three continents. We have global reach and impact but operate fast as we are a relatively small company.
Qualifications
Professional skills & competencies
  • Autonomous, driven, with strong ability to quickly adapt and respond to change.
  • Customer oriented and quality mindset - we continually strive to deliver true customer value.
  • Open-minded, solutions oriented, and a true team player - gaining energy through collaboration with others.
  • Fluent in English (spoken and written).
Technical skills & knowledge
  • Experienced full-stack / backend engineer.
  • Expertise with Java 8 Programming language and frameworks (e.g. Spring, JEE).
  • Experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience in Datastore including data modelling.
  • Good command of Linux operating system.
  • Familiar with collaboration tools for source code management (ie: Git, Bitbucket) and backlog management (ie:Jira, Confluence)
  • Knowledge of ElasticSearch is a plus.
  • Knowledge of continuous delivery process and technologies (e.g. Docker, Jenkins, Ansible) is a plus.
